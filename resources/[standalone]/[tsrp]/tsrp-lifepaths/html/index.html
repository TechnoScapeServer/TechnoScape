Choose Your Lifepath

Corpo

Enter the world of corporate power and influence as a corpo. Navigate the cutthroat landscape of Night City's megacorporations, using your cunning and connections to climb the ladder of success. But beware, as rivals and corporate espionage threaten to undermine your progress

Streetkid

Embrace the gritty streets of Night City as a streetkid Survive through street smarts, connections, and navigating the treacherous urban landscape. Rise above the challenges, form alliances, and let the neon-lit streets become your playground.

Nomad

Venture into the outskirts of Night City as a nomad. Roam the open road, embrace freedom, and navigate the lawless Badlands. Uncover the secrets of your nomadic clan, forge new alliances, and challenge the corporate dominance of the city.

NCPD Officer

Enforce the law in the chaotic streets of Night City as an NCPD officer. Uphold justice, navigate corrupt systems, and battle against rising crime rates. Make tough choices, investigate cases, and fight for a semblance of order in a city on the edge.

Trauma Team

Join the elite ranks of Trauma Team in Night City, the specialized medical response unit. Provide life-saving emergency care to the citizens of Night City, braving danger to extract and protect clients at any cost. Experience high-stakes missions, intense rescue operations, and the moral dilemmas of being on the front lines of healthcare in a dystopian world.
